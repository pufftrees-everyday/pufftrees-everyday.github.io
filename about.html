About the site

Cursed Realm

A community-built companion for Sorcery: Contested Realm — browse every card, build and share decks, and keep the table running.

What this is

Sorcery: Contested Realm is all about fun and imagination, so a few features here are named in the same spirit. If a name doesn't sound familiar, here's the key:

  • Workshop the deck builder
  • Archive where public decks are kept
  • Scrolls deck primers
  • Vault your collection

If you have any questions, let me know!

What you can do here

A handful of tools, each doing one job well.

Search and filter every card across all sets, with four ways to view them and threshold breakdowns at a glance.
